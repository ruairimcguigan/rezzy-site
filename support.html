Rezzy Support

Switch your Mac's display resolution from the menu bar.

Can't see the Rezzy icon? Open the menu anywhere:

⌃+⌥+⌘+D

That's Control + Option + Command + D. macOS sometimes hides menu-bar icons when the bar is full (common on notched Macs) — this shortcut opens Rezzy's menu no matter what, so you can always switch back.

You can also re-open Rezzy from Spotlight or Launchpad. And after any change, Rezzy reverts automatically if you don't confirm — so you can't get stuck on a bad resolution.

Getting started

Rezzy lives in your menu bar — it has no Dock icon and no window. Click the monitor icon at the top-right of your screen to open the menu, then pick a resolution for any connected display. Hover a resolution to preview it; click to apply.

Frequently asked

I launched Rezzy but nothing happened.
Rezzy is a menu-bar app — look for the monitor icon at the top-right of your screen. There is no window or Dock icon. Re-launching it opens the menu.
The Rezzy icon disappeared from my menu bar.
macOS hides menu-bar icons when the bar runs out of room (common on notched Macs, or at some resolutions). Rezzy is still running — press ⌃⌥⌘D to open its menu anywhere, or re-open Rezzy from Spotlight or Launchpad, then pick a resolution that fits. After any change, Rezzy's Keep/Revert prompt also reverts automatically if you don't confirm, so you can't get stuck.
How does the free trial work?
Rezzy is free to use for 7 days. After that, a one-time in-app purchase ("Rezzy Unlock") keeps resolution switching enabled. Your favourites and settings stay.
I bought the unlock on another Mac.
Open the menu and choose Restore Purchase while signed in with the same Apple ID.
A resolution looked wrong — how do I get back?
Rezzy shows a "Keep / Revert" countdown after each change and reverts automatically if you don't confirm. You can also choose Reset to Default for any display.
