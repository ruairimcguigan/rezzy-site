Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

Rezzy does not collect any data. It contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers, and it does not transmit any personal information.

What stays on your Mac

Your preferences — favourite resolutions, custom labels, per-display defaults, and the trial date — are stored locally on your Mac and never leave your device.

Purchases

The optional one-time unlock is handled entirely by Apple's App Store and StoreKit. Rezzy never sees your payment details. Any data involved in a purchase is processed by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy.

Network use

Rezzy makes no network connections of its own. The only network activity is Apple's StoreKit verifying the unlock purchase.
